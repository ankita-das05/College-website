Department of Electronics

Department of Electronics was established in 2003. The department offers B.Sc General course in Electronics (ELTG) with Part I, Part II, and Part III and CBCS system as per University of Calcutta norm.

Laboratory:

The department has a well-equipped laboratory for Analog & Digital Electronics, Tele-communication, Microprocessor & Microcontroller practicals. Computer Science Honours students also share this laboratory for their Hardware practicals.

The department also has a Nanomaterial Processing Research Laboratory with the following equipment facilities:
  • Fumehood
  • Hot Air Oven
  • Microbalance (0.1 mg resolution)
  • Heater with a magnetic stirrer
  • pH meter
  • Ultra-sonicator
  • UV cabinet (365 nm, 256 nm sources)
  • UV-VIS-NIR spectrophoto meter
  • Chiller (up to 5 Degree)
  • Furnace (800 Degree C)
  • DC Power supply (60V, 3A)
  • I-V measurement facility
  • CRO, DSO

computer and other Facilites:

Computers, Printer-scanner-copier, LCD projector based classroom, Wi-Fi Internet facility for research students.
